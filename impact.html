Recent Activities

Our latest interventions around the world

Nairobi, Kenya
June 15, 2023

School Hygiene Program Launch

Launched our comprehensive school hygiene program at 5 primary schools in Nairobi's informal settlements, training 45 teachers and distributing 1,500 hygiene kits to students.

2,100 students reached 6 workshops conducted
Lima, Peru
June 8, 2023

Community Water Filter Installation

Completed installation of 12 community water filtration systems in Lima's most vulnerable neighborhoods, providing clean water access to over 8,000 residents.

12 filtration systems 8,200 beneficiaries
Dhaka, Bangladesh
May 30, 2023

Flood Relief Hygiene Kits

Distributed emergency hygiene kits to 3,500 families affected by seasonal flooding in Dhaka's low-lying areas, preventing waterborne disease outbreaks.

3,500 kits delivered 15 health workers trained
