Frequently Asked Questions

Our hygiene kits are available to individuals and communities in need, with priority given to underserved areas, schools, shelters, and crisis situations. We work to ensure equitable distribution to those who need them most.

Standard hygiene kits are provided free of charge through donor support. In some cases, we may offer subsidized premium kits for organizations that can contribute to the costs.

For individuals, we typically provide 1 kit per person. For organizations and community groups, we assess needs based on the size of your group and available inventory.

Standard Hygiene Kit

The standard kit contains essential personal hygiene items to support basic health and cleanliness for individuals.

For: Individuals
Duration: ~1 month
Weight: 0.8 kg

Usage Instructions

  • Use soap for daily bathing and handwashing
  • Brush teeth twice daily with toothpaste
  • Replace mask after 8 hours of use or when damp
  • Clean nail clippers with alcohol after each use

Family Hygiene Kit

The family kit contains expanded quantities of essential items to support households with multiple members.

For: Families (4-5 people)
Duration: ~2 weeks
Weight: 2.5 kg

Usage Instructions

  • Assign personal items (toothbrushes, razors) to individuals
  • Store soap in a dry place between uses
  • Use shampoo sparingly - a coin-sized amount is sufficient
  • Hang towel to dry completely after each use

Feminine Hygiene Kit

The feminine kit contains specialized items to support menstrual health and personal hygiene for women and girls.

For: Women & Girls
Duration: ~1 cycle
Weight: 0.6 kg

Usage Instructions

  • Change pads every 4-6 hours or when saturated
  • Wash hands before and after changing products
  • Use washcloth with soap for daily cleaning
  • Dispose of used products in provided bag
