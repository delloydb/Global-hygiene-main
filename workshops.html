Clean Water, Healthy Village

Rural Kenya

After our WASH workshop, the community worked together to build proper sanitation facilities and protect their water source. Within months, waterborne illnesses dropped by 70%.

"We didn't realize how our daily habits were making us sick. Now our children are healthier and missing less school."
James Mwangi
Community Elder

From Participant to Trainer

Mumbai, India

Priya attended our workshop as a teenager, then became a certified trainer. She's now taught hygiene practices to over 500 students in her neighborhood.

A School's Transformation

Guatemala City

After implementing our school hygiene program, attendance rates improved by 25% as students stayed healthier. The school now serves as a model for others in the district.

"Our students are more focused in class now that they're not constantly battling preventable illnesses."
Carlos Mendez
School Principal
